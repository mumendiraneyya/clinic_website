# Competitor Analysis Skill

## Purpose
Analyze competitor SEO and GEO strategies to identify opportunities and gaps. Understand what's working in your market and find ways to differentiate.

## Activation
Use when you need to:
- Identify top competitors in search results
- Analyze competitor content strategies
- Find backlink opportunities
- Understand competitor GEO/AI visibility
- Discover competitive gaps

## Process

### Step 1: Identify Competitors

#### Types of Competitors
1. **Direct Competitors**: Same service/product, same market
2. **SERP Competitors**: Ranking for your target keywords (may differ)
3. **AI Competitors**: Being cited by AI for your topics

```
Methods to identify:
- Search target keywords, note top 10 results
- Ask AI assistants about your topic, note cited sources
- Use Ahrefs/SEMrush organic competitors report
- Industry knowledge
```

### Step 3: Content Analysis

#### Content Inventory
```
For each competitor:
- Total indexed pages
- Content types (blog, service pages, FAQs, videos)
- Content frequency (posts per month)
- Average content length
- Topic coverage
- Arabic vs English ratio
- Content freshness (last updated)
```

#### Content Quality Signals
- E-E-A-T indicators (author bios, credentials)
- Original research/data
- Visual content (images, videos, infographics)
- User engagement (comments, shares)
- Citations from other sites

### Step 4: Keyword Gap Analysis

```
1. Export your keyword rankings
2. Export each competitor's keyword rankings
3. Find keywords where:
   - Competitors rank, you don't (gaps)
   - You rank lower than competitors (opportunities)
   - Multiple competitors rank (validated keywords)
   - No one ranks well (blue ocean keywords)
```

#### Arabic Keyword Gaps
Pay special attention to:
- Dialect variations competitors miss
- Long-tail Arabic queries
- Franco-Arab (Arabizi) keywords
- Voice search phrases

### Step 7: SWOT Analysis

```
STRENGTHS (Internal Positive)
- What do you do better than competitors?
- Unique value propositions
- Technical advantages

WEAKNESSES (Internal Negative)
- Where do competitors outperform you?
- Resource limitations
- Technical debt

OPPORTUNITIES (External Positive)
- Gaps competitors haven't filled
- Emerging keywords/topics
- New AI platforms
- Underserved Arabic queries

THREATS (External Negative)
- Competitor advantages
- Market changes
- Algorithm updates
- AI disruption
```

## Tools for Data Collection
- Ahrefs Site Explorer
- SEMrush Domain Overview
- Moz Link Explorer
- SimilarWeb
- SpyFu
- Manual SERP analysis
- AI assistant testing

## Jordan/MENA Specific Considerations

1. **Local Directory Presence**: Check Tebcan, Altibbi, Yellow Pages Jordan
2. **Arabic Content Volume**: Compare Arabic page counts
3. **Local Trust Signals**: Hospital affiliations, ministry recognitions
4. **Regional Backlinks**: .jo domains, regional news sites
5. **Social Proof**: Arabic reviews on Google, Facebook
