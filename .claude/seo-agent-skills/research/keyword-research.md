# Keyword Research Skill

## Purpose
Discover high-value keywords with intent analysis, difficulty scoring, and topic clustering. Special focus on Arabic keywords and dialect variations for Jordan and MENA markets.

## Activation
Use when you need to:
- Find target keywords for a website or page
- Analyze search intent behind keywords
- Discover Arabic keyword opportunities
- Identify dialect-specific keyword variations
- Build topic clusters for content strategy

## Process

### Step 1: Understand the Business
```
- What is the core offering/service?
- Who is the target audience?
- What geographic region? (Jordan, GCC, etc.)
- Primary language(s)?
- What problems does the business solve?
```

### Step 2: Seed Keyword Generation
Generate initial keywords from:
- Business description
- Service/product names
- Common customer questions
- Competitor analysis
- Industry terminology

### Step 3: Arabic Keyword Considerations

#### Dialect Variations by Region
| Meaning | Jordan/Levant | Saudi Arabia | Egypt | Standard Arabic |
|---------|---------------|--------------|-------|-----------------|
| Mobile phone | موبايل | جوال | موبايل | هاتف محمول |
| Car | سيارة | سيارة | عربية | سيارة |
| Now | هلق / هلأ | الحين | دلوقتي | الآن |
| How | كيف | وش / كيف | إزاي | كيف |

#### Spelling Variations
- Formal vs informal spellings (higher volume often with informal)
- With/without diacritics (تشكيل)
- Transliterated English terms (common in tech)

### Step 4: Intent Classification
Classify each keyword by intent:

| Intent | Signal Words | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Informational | ما هو، كيف، لماذا، what is, how to | ما هي أعراض الفتق |
| Navigational | اسم علامة تجارية، brand name | دكتور معمن الدرانية |
| Commercial | أفضل، مقارنة، best, vs | أفضل جراح في عمان |
| Transactional | حجز، شراء، سعر، book, buy | حجز موعد جراحة |

### Step 5: Keyword Metrics to Analyze
```
- Search Volume (monthly)
- Keyword Difficulty (0-100)
- CPC (indicates commercial value)
- SERP Features (featured snippets, local pack, etc.)
- Trend (rising, stable, declining)
```

### Step 6: Topic Clustering
Group keywords into semantic clusters:

```
Example for a surgical clinic:

Cluster: Hernia Surgery (جراحة الفتق)
├── ما هو الفتق
├── أعراض الفتق
├── علاج الفتق بالمنظار
├── تكلفة عملية الفتق في الأردن
├── أفضل دكتور فتق في عمان
└── مضاعفات عملية الفتق
```

### Step 7: Prioritization Matrix

Score keywords on:
| Factor | Weight | Score (1-5) |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Search Volume | 25% | |
| Relevance to Business | 30% | |
| Competition/Difficulty | 20% | |
| Commercial Intent | 15% | |
| Content Gap Opportunity | 10% | |

**Priority Score = Weighted Sum**

## Arabic-Specific Tips

1. **Use both Arabic and transliterated keywords**: Many users search "dr" instead of "دكتور"
2. **Include common misspellings**: Arabic has many spelling variations
3. **Consider Franco-Arab (Arabizi)**: Numbers replacing letters (7 for ح, 3 for ع)
4. **Voice search keywords**: Use conversational Arabic phrases
5. **Local modifiers**: Add city names (عمان، جرش، إربد)

## Example: Medical Clinic Keywords

```
Primary Keywords:
- جراح عام في عمان (general surgeon in Amman)
- دكتور جراحة منظار (laparoscopic surgery doctor)
- عملية فتق بالمنظار الأردن (laparoscopic hernia surgery Jordan)

Long-tail Keywords:
- ما هي تكلفة عملية المرارة بالمنظار في الأردن
- أفضل دكتور جراحة قولون في عمان
- تجربتي مع عملية البواسير

Local Modifiers:
- جراح في شارع مكة
- عيادة جراحة قرب مستشفى الخالدي
```
