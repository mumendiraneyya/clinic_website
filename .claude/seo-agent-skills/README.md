# SEO & GEO Expert Agent

A comprehensive Claude Code skills library for Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO), with specialized knowledge for Arabic content and the Middle East/Jordan market.

## Overview

This agent provides expert-level SEO and GEO consulting capabilities:

- **Traditional SEO**: Technical optimization, on-page SEO, content strategy
- **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**: Optimize for AI systems (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)
- **Arabic & MENA Specialization**: RTL formatting, dialect awareness, cultural optimization
- **Local SEO**: Jordan-focused local search optimization

> "SEO gets you ranked in search results. GEO gets you cited by AI systems."

## Key Capabilities

### Traditional SEO

- **Technical SEO**: Crawlability, indexing, Core Web Vitals, mobile optimization
- **On-Page SEO**: Title tags, meta descriptions, headings, content optimization
- **Content Strategy**: Keyword research, topic clusters, content gap analysis
- **Link Building**: Internal linking, backlink analysis

### Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

- **llms.txt Generation**: AI-readable site summaries
- **Entity Clarity**: Make your business clearly identifiable by AI
- **Content Quotability**: Structure content for AI citation
- **AI Crawler Access**: Proper robots.txt configuration

### Arabic & MENA Specialization

- **RTL Implementation**: Right-to-left formatting
- **Dialect Awareness**: Jordan/Levant, Gulf, Egyptian variations
- **Cultural Optimization**: Ramadan, religious sensitivity, trust signals
- **Local SEO**: Google Business Profile, Jordanian directories

|--------|------|------|
| LCP | ≤2.5s | >4.0s |
| INP | ≤200ms | >500ms |
| CLS | ≤0.1 | >0.25 |

- 88% of MENA searches in Arabic
- <2% of web content in Arabic (opportunity!)

## E-E-A-T Framework

All recommendations follow Google's E-E-A-T:
- **Experience**: First-hand knowledge
- **Expertise**: Subject matter depth
- **Authoritativeness**: Industry recognition
- **Trustworthiness**: Accuracy and transparency
